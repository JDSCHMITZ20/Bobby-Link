About Bobby "Blink" Link

I was born and raised in Chicago now currently residing in Plainfield, Illinois working at Constable Tattoo Parlor. I started my tattooing career 7 years ago in Franklin Park, Illinois and since then I have worked all over the country.

I am a versatile artist that has won several awards! Recently I have been working on sharing my artwork throughout the world through my Twitch.tv platform. I put my passion for art, tattooing, and technology all into one source. Follow me on all my social media platforms above or below and keep up with me on Twitch to see Live Tattooing!
